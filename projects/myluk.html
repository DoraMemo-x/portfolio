MYLUK Posters

Macao Youth League in the United Kingdom

  • Tech Stack: Adobe Illustrator

When I briefly went back to my hometown, I joined the MYLUK (Macau Youth League in the United Kingdom) organisation and volunteered as a graphics designer. I designed three posters in total: two for advertising the biggest event every year - the annual trip, and one for a small bowling social event (although this event was never realised).
